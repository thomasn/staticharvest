Welcome to Shared Harvest

We bake our own bread made from organically grown flour that we mill ourselves to give the best possible flavour - flour that has been milled a while becomes oxidised and loses both flavour and nutrients. We use local honey and other simple ingredients. We invite you to have a look around our website, and if you like what you see, get in touch by phone, email or via the Get In Touch page.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is simple — we wish to share the harvest that nature has blessed us with, grown locally without the use of chemicals, keeping ‘food miles’ minimal.

At Shared Harvest, we bake our own bread and can teach you how to make your own bread too on our Bread Making Courses. We are a family-run business, having lived in the area all our lives and our intention is to provide our local community with good, healthy food.
